#Book #Children

«Mein NEUES altes Haus - Eine Geschichte für Kinder über Umbau statt Neubau»

Korinna Zinovia Weber, Meruert Zharekesheva

A story about renovation instead of demolition. This book for children (age 4+) introduces the topic of sustainability and transformation in architecture to the very little ones.

Published on 23 October 2024, Layflat hardcover, 18 pages, 21 x 21 cm. Available in German, English, French, Italian, Greek, Dutch and Hungarian!

For booklovers in Switzerland: We recommend to buy the German version of the book via the bookstore Hochparterre Bücher Zürich or write us an email (also for other language editions) - we printed a handful number of copies in advance and can ship within Switzerland.
